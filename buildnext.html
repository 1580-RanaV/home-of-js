JAYARAJU’S DUPLEX

This duplex proposal, situated in the heart of Hyderabad city, is designed for a family of 4-6 members. The client requested a residence featuring a formal living room, kitchen, dining area, three bedrooms, and a family living area. Emphasizing the importance of family time, the design includes a cozy family sit-out lawn with landscaping, highlighted by a distinctive striped elevation feature in the background. The design harmoniously combines functionality with aesthetics, creating a warm and inviting home environment that fosters family togetherness.

DR.
PRAKASH’S VILLA

This large-scale proposal, located in the historic province of Hyderabad city, is designed for a family of 7-8 members. The client’s brief included a residence featuring a formal living room, kitchen, dining area, five bedrooms, a family living area, and a gym. A key highlight of the project is the exterior family deck, which is enhanced by landscaped stairs leading to the main entrance. A notable challenge was raising the plinth level to a height of 7 to 9 feet, which required careful planning and execution.

SUMAN’S VILLA

This modern villa proposal, tailored for a family of 5-6 members, is situated in the emerging residential hub of Hyderabad.
